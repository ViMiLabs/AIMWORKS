Deprecation Policy

Rules for deprecating, replacing, and migrating ontology terms without silently breaking public identifiers.

H2KG treats deprecation as an explicit release-governed activity rather than a silent cleanup step.

  • Deprecation should be explicit, reviewable, and release-governed rather than hidden in silent rewrites.
  • Deprecated terms should remain resolvable, be marked with deprecation annotations, and point to replacements where possible.
  • Breaking namespace changes must be documented with redirect rules, migration notes, and prior-version links.
  • Terms that remain local by design should be documented as intentional, not treated as temporary errors.
